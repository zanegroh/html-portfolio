The Best Movies According to Zane

My top 3 movies of all time

Moonlight

A deeply intimate and visceral movie, Moonlight prods at our most sensitive parts. Every scene is woven with the passionate emotion that leaves you continually dumbfounded that you are watching a movie and not witnessing first-hand a forlorn family affair.

Whiplash

Sometimes perfection is just not good enough. How do young minds reckon with their undetermined identity in a changing world? The arts require a delicacy and sensitivity that is ever so easy to shatter and must be intently nurtured like the most velvety of succulents in the driest of climates. What will an aspiring drummer do, in the midst of a single parent household, adaptation to college life, and the most stunningly insurmountable obstacle yet to confront him: his music teacher.

Oppenheimer

An epic piece of cinematography whose lengthy production seemed to captivate the general public, Oppenheimer is one of the most awaited films of all time. For good reason - it, in quite ambitious manner, attempts a relation of the life of one of the most consequential men to live, Robert Oppenheimer. A man who completed feats that objectively changed our world. The question is: was it for good or bad?
